Aftovaxpur™

  • Inactivated and purified active ingredients adjuvanted with aluminium hydroxide and saponin, generating immunization in cattle, sheep and goats under subcutaneous route.

  • Highly potent vaccine (at least 6 PD50).

  • 24 months shelf life.

Aftopor™

  • Inactivated and purified active ingredients formulated in a double oil emulsion (W/O/W), generating immunization in pigs, cattle, sheep and goats under intramuscular route.

  • Highly potent vaccine (at least 6 PD50).

  • 18 months shelf life.
