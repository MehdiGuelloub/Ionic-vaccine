A dedicated team of Veterinary Professionals at the service of Foot-and-Mouth Disease control and preparedness.

Merial VPH Technical Services assists governments, veterinary services, veterinarians and livestock producers in their efforts to control Foot-and-Mouth Disease through scientific advice, laboratory and diagnostic testing. Our staff of Technical Service Professionals, composed of veterinarians fully knowledgeable about all the aspects of FMD and our products, is available to answer questions about our products and provide valuable scientific input into FMD control programs.

Our dedicated FMD Technical Service Lab provides specialized laboratory support in the field of diagnostics, vaccine matching and post-vaccination monitoring. Based in Lelystad, it works closely with the R&D team, creating relevant synergies. The FMD Technical Service Lab partners with other laboratories and offers training programs on standard FMD techniques as well as participation to proficiency tests.
